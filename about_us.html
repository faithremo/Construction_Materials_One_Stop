About Us

Construction Materials One Stop is an online store bringing together suppliers of building and construction materials in one place. Founded in 2021, Construction Materials One Stop is the leading provider of building materials in East and Central Africa, with headquarters in Nairobi, Kenya. Our diverse team of professionals has years of experience in the building and construction industry making us your best bet on everything you need to move your building and construction design from paper to reality.

Why We Do What We Do

We believe the building and construction process does not have to be complicated; it should be as simple as possible. For the buyer, we simplify the construction process for you by giving you a wide selection of suppliers in one place for all your construction needs. As a seller, we simplify the customer acquisition process by bringing the customer to you to simplify your sales and marketing efforts.

Why You Should Choose Us

Trusted Suppliers

All sellers on the site are verified registered businesses who have passed all the checks we have put on the site before beginning any transactions.

Transparent Pricing

On the site, you can compare pricing from different suppliers and determine who is the best fit for your needs.

Secure Payment Options

All payments pass through our secure platform ensuring only you have access to your payment information.

Assured Quality

We perform random checks on feedback from our buys to ensure our suppliers provide only the best quality of building and construction material.
Our customer service team investigates all complaints associated with quality concerns. Any supplier not meeting quality standards is removed from the site.

Stress-free Construction

We bring you building and construction materials buyers and sellers in one place taking the hassle out of the buying and selling process. Buy and sell
with just one click.
